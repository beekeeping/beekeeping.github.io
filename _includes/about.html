About company

We produce pure raw honey straight from our hives to your table across the Georgia and beyond.

Beekeeping has been a part of our families for over 50 years. All of us are committed to providing the highest quality honey.

It is a well-known fact that the flowers, herbs and blossoms on which the honey bees feed on is a key contributing factor to the unique and complex flavors of honey. Our honey is pure, unfiltered and never adulterated.
